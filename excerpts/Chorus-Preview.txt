Badnam ishq ch main bolda haan out loud,
Tenu vekh ke main bhullda haan the whole crowd.
Tere pyaar da saroor chadeya, feeling alive,
Main tere naal chalda haan to survive.
Teri ankh de ishaare utte taking a chance,
Main vi zindagi nu jeenda haan with a glance.
Mere sahaan vich tu vassdi, holding me tight,
Main tere layi lad-da haan in the night.
