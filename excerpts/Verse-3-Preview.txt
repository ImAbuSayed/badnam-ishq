Pehlan sochiya main jitda haan every game,
Hun tere layi bhullda haan fortune and fame.
Kaale rang di jacket ch hiding my pain,
Main vi hanjuan nu rokda haan in the rain.
Tu te apne raste turr gayi looking away,
Main vi patthran te likhda haan what I say.
Zehar ishq da peenda haan bitter taste,
Main te apni jawani nu karda ni waste.
