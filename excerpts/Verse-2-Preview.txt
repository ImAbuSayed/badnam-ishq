Engine di ghurrahat sunda haan shifting gears,
Main vi raste te rukda nai chasing my fears.
Leather di seat te behnda haan feeling the rush,
Tere ditte hue zakhm vi karde ne crush.
Main te yaaran naal khad-da haan breaking the wall,
Tere karke main arrda haan risking it all.
Saddi yaari da rutba hai high level,
Main te ishq ch ban gaya haan rebel.
