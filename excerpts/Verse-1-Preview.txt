Dhuyein di lakeer ch takkda haan cold desire,
Mere seene vich balda haan, raging fire.
Dhundli jehi roshni ch standing so tall,
Tere ishq ch main jagda haan catching the fall.
Tu vi kardi ishaare, got me going crazy,
Main vi piche naiyo hatda haan, never lazy.
Tu chori chori takkdi ae, hiding your smile,
Main vi seene vich rakhda haan my own style.
